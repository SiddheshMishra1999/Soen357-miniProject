Eat Locally!

By: Siddhesh Mishra, Shriman Vinyagamoorty, Devin Kuriya and Mitchel Scott-Bond

Introduction

The Problem

When Covid began and quarantine was enforced, many people switched to ordering food online, this was a great time for local restaurants. But now that covid has ended people are slowly but surely going back to their old habits of ordering takeout from big restaurants. This is a big problem for local restaurants as they are not doing as well as they used to and this could be very bad for buisiness on the long run.
